About OSAS

Supporting students beyond the classroom.

The Office of Student Affairs and Services (OSAS) is committed to the holistic development of every Polytechnician — academically, socially, and personally. We design programs and services that respond to real student needs, while creating a campus culture that is inclusive, safe, and inspiring.

From scholarships and guidance to leadership and wellness, OSAS is the bridge between the student experience and the institution that serves it.

Student Welfare

Guidance, counseling, and wellness programs that put students first.

Leadership & Engagement

Co-curricular and extra-curricular opportunities that build character.

Scholarships & Assistance

Information, processing, and follow-through for student grants.

"Our work is simple — make sure every student feels seen, supported, and set up to succeed."

— OSAS Tagudin Campus
